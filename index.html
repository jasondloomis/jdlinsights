Dog Company - 3/509th Infantry (Airborne)

Dog Company is one of five Airborne companies belonging to the 3rd battalion 509th Infantry Regiment at Fort Richardson, Alaska. The unit and company have a long Airborne history starting in World War II when they were the first Airborne combat unit in the Army.

Currently, Dog Company is a heavy weapons mounted airborne infantry company. The unit was reactivated in 2004 and has deployed to Iraq and Afghanistan multiple times for the Global War on Terrorism Campaign.
