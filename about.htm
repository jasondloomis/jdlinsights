From the Author

Thank you for visiting my site. My name is Jason and I am currently retired from the U.S. Army Airborne Infantry and am attending college at SNHU to complete my Bachelors in Information Technology concentration Database Management. I created this site for my Web Design class final project.

Over my 20 year career in the Army I was stationed at multiple duty stations all over the United States and abroad. I also completed several overseas tours that included South Korea, Haiti, Kuwait, Afghanistan, Panama and Thailand.

This website was created by a retired U.S. Army Paratrooper for school purposes.
