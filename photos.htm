Afghanistan

The 3rd Battalion, 509th Infantry (Airborne) deployed to Afghanistan twice. These pictures are from Operation Enduring Freedom 09/10 and 12/13. In 2009/2010 Dog Company was in the Khowst Province of Afghanistan protecting the KG (Khowst to Gardez Pass) and the Afghan and Pakistan border. The rest of the Battalion was in the Paktika Province conducting a separate mission.

In 2012/2013 deployment the Battalion deployed to the Paktia Province and was headquartered in the city of Gardez. Headquarters and Dog companies were positioned for operations in Gardez which sits approximately 7650 feet above sea level in the Hindu-Kush Mountains.
